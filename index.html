The "Invest Then Test" Strategy

Here's the strategy that changes everything: Instead of giving everything upfront and hoping for the best, you make small investments and test if she reciprocates before investing more.

The Game-Changing Approach

Instead of immediately planning elaborate dates after getting her number, send a simple test message first:

Hey, it was great getting to know you.

This simple text reveals her true interest level. If she responds favorably, THEN you invest energy in planning something. If she doesn't respond well or takes forever to reply, you've saved yourself time, money, and emotional investment.

Reading Her True Interest Level

One of the biggest nice guy mistakes is misreading polite behavior as romantic interest. Here's how to accurately gauge if she's actually interested:

High Interest Signals

  • Quick responses to your messages (within a few hours)
  • Asks questions about your life and remembers details
  • Initiates contact sometimes - she texts you first occasionally
  • Makes time for you even when she's busy
  • Suggests hanging out or mentions things you could do together
  • Uses flirty language - emojis, playful teasing, compliments
  • Shares personal information about her life, feelings, experiences
  • Physical touch - touches your arm, hugs, sits close

Low Interest Signals

  • Slow responses (days to reply, if at all)
  • Short, unenthusiastic answers - "yeah," "ok," "cool"
  • Never initiates contact - you're always texting first
  • Always busy when you suggest meeting up
  • Doesn't ask questions about your life or interests
  • Formal language - no emojis, no playfulness, sounds robotic
  • Doesn't share personal details - keeps conversation surface-level
  • Avoids physical contact - steps away, no touching
üí° Pro Tip: The 2-Text Rule

If you send two texts in a row without getting a response, stop texting. A woman who's interested will eventually reach out. If she doesn't, you have your answer.

The Energy Match Test

Here's a simple way to gauge interest: Match her energy level. If she sends short texts, you send short texts. If she takes 6 hours to respond, you take 6 hours to respond. If she's putting in minimal effort, don't compensate with extra effort.
